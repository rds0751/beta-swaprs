About

Swaprs is an online marketplace that makes it easy, quick and effective to swap virtually all items and services. Simply upload stuff you are willing to give, discover what people are willing to offer in return, and send or receive ‘swap requests' with other users.

In the UK alone, 300,000 tonnes of wearable clothing, 22 million small items of furniture and 28 million toys are thrown away each year. This is absurd. Our mission is to get people to share more and keep useful stuff away from landﬁlls.

We're launching soon and would love your support to help us create a better and greener economy. Drop us your email so we can let you know when we launch!
